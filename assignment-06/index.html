Five Minute Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 1(.25oz) Package Active Dry Yeast
  • 2 1/2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 2 Tablespoon Honey or 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Cornmeal for dusting pan

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450o F.
  2. In a large bowl disolve yeast and honey or sugar in warm water (110oF) until yeast becomes frothy
  3. Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Stir with a wooden spoon until soft dough forms. The dough should be fairly firm. If it is not mix in an additional tablespoon of flour at a time until no longer sticky. If it is too dry, mix in an additional tablespoon of water.
  4. Roll dough into a ball (for thick crust) or into two seperate balls (for thin crust), or into four separate balls (for indivitual pizzas or extra thin pizzas)
  5. Let the dough rest for 3-5 minutes while you gather your pizza toppings. If desired store dough coated in zip lock bag with olive oil for up to 3 days, or in the freezer for up to a month.
  6. Roll dough into desired thickness and shape. Top with your favorite pizza toppings and bake for 8-10 minutes with a pizza pan dusted with cornmeal until browned and bubbly.
